Buzzwords Vocabulary App

A case study on my process of developing a vocabulary learning mobile application, which provides busy students with a tool to study new terms and concepts efficiently while on the go. During this independent project, I conducted a competitive analysis, user research, as well as created user personas to help me design and create a more human-friendly mobile app. Once I entered the design phase, I began designing the information architecture by creating user flows in Draw.io and Lucidchart. Once I had an understanding of my navigation, I created low-fidelity wireframes to sketch out my ideas. Due to the nature of this project, I had limited time and resources. This is why I created hand-drawn wireframes since this style is quick, efficient, and easy to make changes. From there, I created a prototype of the app to begin user testing. I collected the feedback and created a usability report to organize my findings, and ranked them by severity. I made iterations based on the feedback to my prototype, and it is ready for additional testing.
