About

My Story

Hi, I’m Alisha, and I am a UX/UI designer with a background in business operations management. I offer extensive experience in marketing, sales, database and account management, product design, and customer service. Optimizing work processes while maintaining empathy for the customer has always been a passion of mine. I believe design should always be user-centered, while simultaneously catering to the business goals. I take pride in my ability to identify customer pain points, and present logical, lucrative solutions for clients in the form of concepts and products. I am eager to continue making a difference with innovative and progressive teams that take pride in improving the experience of it’s clients.

While design and business go hand-in-hand, it is not always easy to find synergy between user needs and business goals. As an empathetic designer with a business background, I find a way to harmonize the two without skipping a beat. My services stretch out beyond UX/UI, providing a deep understanding of business operations and product development. I leverage my creativity and background to design original concepts inspired by user needs. With a data-driven approach, I can help you to design and create innovative products that can stand up against top competitors.
